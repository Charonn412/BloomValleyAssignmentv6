Growing Together

Bloom Valley Nursery is more than a shop—it’s a community of neighbors who love to grow, learn, and share.

Workshops & Events

Join us for hands-on learning, seasonal inspiration, and time spent with fellow plant lovers.

Beginner Houseplant Care

First Saturday of each month • 10:00–11:30 a.m.

Learn how to choose the right plants for your home, understand watering and light, and keep your plants thriving.

Includes a mini plant care guide you can take home.

Designing Container Gardens

Second Thursday • 6:00–7:30 p.m.

Discover how to layer height, color, and texture to create containers that look full and balanced all season long.

Workshop fee includes soil and use of tools; plants sold separately.

Pollinator-Friendly Backyards

Last Sunday • 2:00–3:30 p.m.

Explore plant choices and simple changes that support bees, butterflies, and birds in your own backyard.

Perfect for families and new gardeners.

To reserve a spot, call us at (555) 123-4567 or mention the workshop at checkout.

Community Involvement

We’re proud to support local schools, small businesses, and neighborhood spaces.

School Garden Partnerships

Each spring, we work with nearby schools to help students plant and care for learning gardens. We provide seedlings, soil, and basic tools at reduced cost.

Educators can email schools@bloomvalleynursery.com for details.

Neighborhood Green Spaces

From traffic islands to community planters, we donate plants and time to help keep shared spaces lush and inviting.

Have a project in mind? Reach out—we love collaborating.

Local Business Collaborations

We partner with local cafés, boutiques, and studios to create plant styling that enhances their brand and welcomes guests.

Ask about our ongoing plant refresh program for businesses.
