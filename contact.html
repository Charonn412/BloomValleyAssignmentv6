About Bloom Valley Nursery

Bloom Valley Nursery started as a small family greenhouse and has grown into a welcoming destination for plant lovers across the valley. We specialize in curated collections that feel modern, warm, and timeless—whether you are styling a single windowsill or transforming your entire backyard.

Our team is passionate about making gardening approachable. We take time to understand your space, light, and lifestyle so that every plant we recommend has the best chance to thrive.
